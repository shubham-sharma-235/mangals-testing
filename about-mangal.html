    We work for you since 1980.
    Industrial around the world.

    Established in 1990, MEIL is an ISO 9001:2015 certified leader in manufacturing CRGO products and manufacturing transformers. We are approved by Power Grid Corporation of India Limited for 400 kV Class Projects.

    We are processors of CRGO Slit Coils, Cut Laminations, Wound Core & Windings, Amorphous Core, CRGO Core Assembly & CRGO Core Coil Assembly, serving around the world electrical industry with unparalleled expertise and innovation.
