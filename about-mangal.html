    • About Mangal

    What we do

    Our mission is to
    Make energy accessible

    We are in the business of processing transformer components transformer lamination, CRGO slit coils, amorphous cores, coil assemblies and core assemblies, wound core, toroidal core and oil immersed circuit breakers.

    We also trade CRGO and CRNO coils, as well as amorphous ribbons. Further, we manufacture transformers and customised products in the power infrastructure industry. We manufacture transformers with capacities ranging from single-phase 5 KVA to three-phase 10MVA (medium power) units and also provide EPC services for setting up electrical sub-station, catering to the power infrastructure sector

    Our Mission

    Under the leadership of our Founder and Managing Director, Mr. Rahul Mangal, our mission is to establish Mangal Electrical Industries Limited as the premier global manufacturer and distributor of CRGO and transformer accessories.

    . Our Legacy

    Our Journey Through Time:
    The Story Behind Our Legacy

    From humble beginnings to industry leadership, our journey is a testament to innovation, dedication, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Each milestone we achieve adds to the legacy we've built over the years.

    1990

    Humble Beginning

    Mangal Electrical Industries was founded and begin its journey in transformer industry

    2008

    First Milestone

    Launched our transformer manufacturing plant, transitioned to a private limited company, and achieved our first CRGO Cut Lamination and transformer billing on 22nd April 2008.

    2012

    First Amorphous Billing

    Achieved our first billing of Amorphous Core Transformers, marking a significant step in advancing energy-efficient solutions.

    2013

    Expansion to Reengus

    Established the Reengus plant to boost production capacity and meet growing industry demands.

    2014

    Acquisition of Powertech SEZ

    Acquired the Powertech SEZ plant to enhance manufacturing capabilities and expand operational strength.

    2015

    Major Milestones and Growth

    Reached a significant milestone by exporting to DDPL, achieved ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certifications, established a manufacturing unit at Road No. 5, VKI Area, and launched our EPC Division.

    2016

    ICB Manufacturing Initiated

    Commenced the production of Insulated Core Blocks (ICB), expanding our product portfolio and technological capabilities.

    2017

    First ICB Billing

    Achieved our first billing for Insulated Core Blocks (ICB), marking a new milestone in our manufacturing journey.

    2021

    132kV Project Success

    Successfully delivered the 132kV project for PGCIL, showcasing our expertise in high-voltage projects, while also achieving ISO 9001:2015 registration.

    2022

    ISO 14001:2015 Certification

    Achieved ISO 14001:2015 certification, reinforcing our commitment to environmental management and sustainability in operations.

    2023

    ZED Silver Certification

    Received ZED Silver approval, reflecting our dedication to excellence in manufacturing and operational practices.

    2024

    Transition to Public Limited

    Transitioned from a Private Limited to a Public Limited company, marking a significant step in our growth journey.
