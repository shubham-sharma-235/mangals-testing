    About Immersed Circuit Breaker (ICB)

    With 25 years of rich expertise in manufacturing, Mangal Electrical Industries Ltd. specialises in Immersed Circuit Breakers (ICBs). Each set of ICBs includes a breaker, indicator light, connecting rod, lock pin, and handle. Designed with top-notch technology and high-grade raw materials, our circuit breakers are highly sensitive, easy to operate, and offer superior performance and maximum working life. We provide various ranges of circuit breakers tailored to client requirements, featuring efficient voltage capabilities to meet diverse industrial needs.

    Our circuit breakers are constructed with a metal frame in a compartmentalised pattern and have an air-insulated nature. The oil-immersed design effectively reduces damage levels and significantly lowers maintenance costs for distribution transformers. This makes them an ideal choice for ensuring the longevity and reliability of power distribution systems. The strong construction and precise engineering of our ICBs enable them to perform optimally even under challenging environmental conditions, providing consistent protection and reliability.

    Wound Core FAQs

    What are the Features of Immersed Circuit Breaker ?
    Features of Immersed Circuit Breaker are as follows: Efficient compartmentalised design, Dust-resistant construction, Stable operating voltage, Suitable for distribution transformers.
    Are Immersed Circuit Breakers suitable for both residential and industrial applications ?
    Yes, Immersed Circuit Breakers are versatile and can be used in residential and industrial settings. They offer reliable protection for electrical systems in various environments.
    What safety features are incorporated into Immersed Circuit Breakers ?
    Immersed Circuit Breakers incorporate safety features such as overload protection, short circuit protection, and earth leakage protection. They are designed to ensure the safety of electrical systems and prevent damage to equipment and personnel.
    Are there any environmental considerations when using Immersed Circuit Breakers ?
    Yes, Immersed Circuit Breakers use insulating oil, which may have environmental implications if not handled or disposed of properly. To minimise environmental impact, it's important to adhere to proper maintenance and disposal procedures.
