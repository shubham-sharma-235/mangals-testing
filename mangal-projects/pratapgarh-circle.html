    Project Overview

    The 220/132/33KV Dataganj Substation Project in Dataganj, Budaun, Uttar Pradesh, is a major initiative to upgrade the region’s power infrastructure. Commissioned by the Uttar Pradesh Power Transmission Corporation Ltd. (UPPTCL), this project focuses on constructing a modern, SAS-based 220/132/33KV AIS substation. The implementation of this advanced substation automation system aims to significantly enhance the capacity and reliability of the local power transmission network, ensuring a stable and efficient electricity supply.

    Valued at Rs. 23,99,64,297.00, the project highlights the commitment to meeting the growing energy demands of the region. The substation was successfully commissioned, and the process of handing over the project to UPPTCL is currently underway. This development is critical for improving power distribution in Dataganj and surrounding areas, supporting both present and future energy needs while contributing to the overall economic growth and stability of the region.
