    CRGO Slit Coils

    CRGO Slit Coils, also known as Cold Rolled Grain Oriented Steel Slit Coils, are electrical steel products that are widely used in the manufacturing of transformers, inductors, and other power electronic devices. These coils are made by slitting cold-rolled grain-oriented steel sheets or mother coils into narrower strips, which are then wound into coils. The cold rolling process aligns the crystal grains in the steel, significantly improving the material's magnetic properties.

    CRGO Coils offer high magnetic permeability, low core loss, and excellent insulation properties, making them ideal for applications that require high efficiency and low losses. These coils are available in mother form in various sizes (900mm to 1250mm), width shapes, and thicknesses (0.18mm to 0.35mm), allowing manufacturers to choose the right product for their specific needs.
