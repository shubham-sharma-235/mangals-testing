    • Pratapgarh Circle

    . Mangal

    Worked with some
    Extemed Clients

    The Pratapgarh Circle Project, under the ROSS scheme by Ajmer Vidyut Vitran
    Nigam Limited, aims to modernize electricity distribution in Pratapgarh,
    Rajasthan. With an investment of Rs. 1,01,94,99,990.00, it focuses on
    enhancing reliability, efficiency, and sustainability through advanced
    infrastructure and eco-friendly solutions.

    Project Information

    Name of Client:

    Ajmer Vidyut Vitran Nigam Limited, Ajmer

    Name of Project:

    Development of Distribution Infrastructure

    Location:

    Pratapgarh Circle of Ajmer

    Current Status:

    Two 33/11KV substations have been successfully commissioned,
    and work on the 11KV feeders is ongoing.
